Frequently Asked Questions

Why are updates important?

Software updates fix security holes that hackers can use to access your computer, steal your information, or install harmful programs. Installing updates is one of the easiest ways to protect yourself online.

Will updates slow down my computer?

Usually no. While your computer might be slower during the installation, updates often improve performance. Outdated software can actually slow down your computer more than keeping it updated.

How long do updates take?

Most updates take between 5-30 minutes, depending on the size and your internet speed. Critical security updates are usually quick. Larger updates (like Windows feature updates) may take longer.

Can I postpone updates?

You can postpone optional and important updates for a few days. However, we strongly recommend installing critical updates as soon as possible ‚Äî hackers actively exploit these security holes.
