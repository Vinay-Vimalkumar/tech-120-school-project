Quick Safety Tips

  • 1
    Keep Everything Updated
    Updates often fix security holes that hackers can exploit. When you see an update notification, install it soon!
  • 2
    Think Before You Click
    Be suspicious of emails asking you to click links or provide personal information, even if they look official.
  • 3
    Use Strong Passwords
    Create long, unique passwords for each account. Consider using a password manager to keep track of them.
  • 4
    When in Doubt, Verify
    If something seems suspicious, contact the company directly using their official phone number or website — not the one in the email.
