What is Phishing?

Phishing is when criminals send fake emails pretending to be from trusted companies (like your bank, Amazon, or Netflix) to trick you into giving them your personal information, passwords, or money.

What To Do If You Receive a Suspicious Email

  1. Don't click any links in the email
  2. Don't download attachments or open files
  3. Don't reply or provide any information
  4. Contact the company directly using the phone number on your card or their official website (type it yourself, don't use links from the email)
  5. Report the email as spam in your email program, then delete it
